Canada is getting expensive

Despite median wages nearly matching values from thirty years ago, almost a third of Canadians plan on buying a home in the next two years.

With the rising housing costs, a majority of first time home buyers have to extend their wait, with almost a third are relying on family for help.

But it did not always used to be so unaffordable, Canada thirty years ago was a far different place.
